What is a Video Editor?

A video editor is software or tool that allows you to cut, trim, add effects, and enhance videos. Popular options include Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and free alternatives like DaVinci Resolve.

Video Editing Tips

  • Export in high quality (e.g., 1080p or 4K).
